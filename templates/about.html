About Us

CareAge is a platform designed to connect Canadian youth with the elderly population. Our goal is to provide assistance with everyday tasks while fostering intergenerational relationships.

We aim to address the growing needs of Canada's aging population, particularly the baby boomers, by engaging youth in community service. This initiative not only helps the elderly with their daily tasks but also provides valuable experience and volunteer hours for young Canadians.

Our platform facilitates connections for various services, including snow shoveling, lawn mowing, changing light bulbs, and more. By bridging the gap between generations, we create a stronger, more connected community.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between generations while providing essential support to the elderly and valuable experiences for youth.

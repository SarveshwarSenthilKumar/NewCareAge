Volunteer

Are you a youth looking to make a difference in your community? Join CareAge to:

  • Help seniors with everyday tasks
  • Earn volunteer hours
  • Gain valuable experience
  • Bridge the generational gap

How it works:

  1. Sign up as a volunteer
  2. Browse available tasks in your area
  3. Choose tasks that fit your schedule and skills
  4. Complete tasks and earn points
  5. Redeem points for volunteer hours certificates

  • ✅ Make a real difference in your community
